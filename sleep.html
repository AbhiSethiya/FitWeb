💡 Recovery Tips
💧
Hydrate Well
Drink 2–3 litres of water daily. Muscles recover faster when well hydrated.
😴
7–9 Hours of Sleep
Growth hormone peaks during deep sleep — this is when muscle repair happens.
🥩
Protein Timing
Consume 20–40 g of protein within 30 min post-workout to maximise synthesis.
🧊
Cold Exposure
A cold shower or ice bath reduces muscle soreness and inflammation significantly.
🚶
Active Recovery
Light walking or swimming on rest days boosts blood flow without taxing muscles.
📵
Screen-Free Wind-Down
Avoid screens 1 hour before bed. Blue light suppresses melatonin production.
