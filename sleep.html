💡 Sleep Tips

🕐
Maintain a consistent sleep schedule

Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, even on weekends.

📵
Avoid screens 1 hour before bed

Blue light from devices suppresses melatonin production and delays sleep onset.

🌡️
Keep bedroom cool (65–68°F / 18–20°C)

A cooler environment helps trigger the natural drop in core body temperature needed for sleep.

☕
Avoid caffeine after 2 PM

Caffeine has a half-life of ~5 hours; afternoon consumption can disrupt sleep quality.

🏃
Exercise regularly but not close to bedtime

Regular exercise improves sleep, but intense workouts within 2 hours of bedtime may increase alertness.
